和sex & the city这节课有关的一点废话

2018-04-14
#社会化产物

虽然老师的确很努力地在教学生critical thinking，比如在playboy那章，我就从一开始“这是男权社会的糟粕！”到后来觉得把它说成“女性的性解放”也未尝不可。从章头学到章尾，经常从非黑即白的状态到两面论 多面论。但我总感觉老师自己的lecture一直有种嘲讽的语气…
“Alex- the femme fatale- is aggressive, independent, career hungry and goes by a traditionally male name.”
“Again we see a comparison with the “dangerous” life of sex and the city verses the country and a “safe” patriarchal family life.”
“But as feminist film theorists have argued her demise also speaks to the idea of a woman who tries to live outside these prescribed boundaries and what the ultimate price is for them- the inability to survive.”
“According to the male gaze, the woman is always the object.”
女性主义本身是客观的理论，可是一讲出来就不免带有对现实的嘲讽。只能说现实世界实在太荒唐了。

还有一个地方让我觉得我永远不可能真正客观。老师总是在quiz的T/F选择题里给人一些很美好的幻想，比如
“In the 1950’s, the common image of women on TV was the independent and sexually free.”
“The women of playboy had complete control over their careers, in other words they were not measured by others standards in terms of their bodies.”
“Cautionary Tales, such as Valley of the Dolls, celebrated the successful lives of single women in the city and showed how women can resist a patriarchal society and triumph.”
这种一连串下来全选false的很明显的送分题。作为题目来讲，因为这些描述与事实不符所以我选false；可是我作为女权主义者，看到这些难免会感叹，如果这是现实就好了…

每个人都没有办法将那些属于人的东西完全从自身剥离，不仅有male gaze，还有最根本性的human gaze，”When you look at an object, you are seeing the relation between the thing and yourself.”
